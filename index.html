Steak Recipe

Description

Steak is something you can never go wrong with. It is an essential dish that everyone should know how to make. It's easy, delicious and perfect for any occasion. I will help you make steak that will make you never want to order at a restraunt ever again.

Ingredients

  • Steak (1.5-2 inches thick)
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • 2-4 Cloves of Garlic, crushed left in skin
  • 1 Bunch of Fresh Thyme
  • 4 Tablespoons of Unsalted Butter

Steps

  1. Let Steak rest to room temp
  2. Pat steak with paper towel to dry off steak
