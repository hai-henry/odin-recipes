Air Fried Crispy Potatoes

Description

Steak is something you can never go wrong with. It is an essential dish that everyone should know how to make. It's easy, delicious and perfect for any occasion. I will help you make steak that will make you never want to order at a restraunt ever again.

Ingredients

  • Creamy Potatoes
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powderr
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Cut the creamy potatoes in half
  2. Coat the potatoes with olive oil
  3. Mix in all your seasonings
  4. Air fry the potatoes at 400 degrees fahrenheit for 10 minutes
  5. When done, check and stir the potaotes
  6. If the potatoes are how you like it, you are done
  7. If not, air fry for another 8-10 minutes at 400 degrees fahrenheit depending on how crispy you like them
